About Cortis and the Glowroom Fandom

Our history, mission, and the meaning behind the name.

The Group: CORTIS

Cortis (코르티스) is a five-member K-Pop boy group formed by Big-Hit(HYBE) Entertainment. They debuted on August 18, 2025, with their highly anticipated single, "Glow Up." The name Cortis is derived from the Latin word meaning "Courage" and the concept of a shining star, symbolizing their bold energy and bright future in the industry.

The Fandom: GLOWROOM

The official fandom name is COER. Just as light requires a dark room to truly shine, the fandom provides the supportive space where Cortis can showcase their talents and grow. Our community is built on positivity, enthusiasm, and a commitment to streaming and voting! This website is the central hub for all Glowroom activities.

Our Mission

To celebrate the music and talent of Cortis, provide the fastest and most accurate updates, and foster a friendly, global community where every fan feels like they belong. We believe in healthy fandom culture and mutual respect.
